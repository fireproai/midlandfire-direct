Gent by Honeywell / Nano / Fire Panel Accessories

Nano accessories and ancillary items

Accessories for Gent Nano are selected to support installation, indication, and integration with related building services. In distributor terms, this category covers typical items specified alongside the control panel to complete a compliant installation and simplify maintenance. Selection depends on the project’s cause-and-effect requirements as defined by the system design.

Common accessory types include installation accessories, interface modules, and ancillary items for local indication and external signalling. These are used to adapt the system to site conditions, connect monitored circuits, and provide required connections to third-party equipment. Fire alarm professionals generally choose accessories based on the risk assessment, cabling approach, and the building’s operational needs.

  • Installation accessories for mounting, cable management, and tidy panel-area finishing
  • Interface modules for monitoring inputs and providing control outputs to ancillary plant or fire protection equipment
  • Ancillary items for local indication, remote signalling, and practical handover requirements
  • Add-ons typically specified to suit site-specific integration points and service access needs
